Artur Märchen

Artur Märchen (Ernst Erich Artur Raake), painter and graphic artist. Märchen was born on November 7, 1932 in Grünberg / Silesia. He died on March 1, 2002 in Berlin. More information (in German) on museum-digial:berlin or in the Wikipedia.
